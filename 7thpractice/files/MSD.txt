Mahendra Singh Dhoni, commonly known as MS Dhoni, is a former Indian cricketer and one of the most successful captains in international cricket. Born on July 7, 1981, in Ranchi, India, Dhoni is known for his calm demeanor, exceptional leadership, and finishing skills as a wicketkeeper-batsman. He captained India to victory in the 2007 ICC T20 World Cup, the 2011 ICC Cricket World Cup, and the 2013 ICC Champions Trophy. Dhoni is also famous for his helicopter shot and is widely regarded as one of the greatest wicketkeepers in cricket history. He retired from international cricket in 2020.
